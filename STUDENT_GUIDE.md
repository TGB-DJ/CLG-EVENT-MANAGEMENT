Welcome to the EventFlow Portal! Here is how you can access your dashboard and tickets.

## 1. Access the Portal
*   Visit the website URL provided by your college (e.g., `https://your-college.github.io/event-manager`).
*   You can also install the app on your phone by clicking "Add to Home Screen" if prompted.

## 2. Sign In
1.  On the Login Page, click the **"Sign in / Sign up with Google"** button.
2.  Select your **College Email ID** (or personal Google account if allowed).
    *   *Note: If this is your first time, an account will be automatically created for you.*

## 3. Your Dashboard
Once logged in, you will be redirected to the **Student Portal**.
*   **Profile**: You can see your name and email at the top.
*   **My Registrations**: A list of all events you have registered for.
*   **Status**:
    *   ðŸŸ¡ **Registered**: You are signed up but haven't attended yet.
    *   ðŸŸ¢ **Attended**: Your QR code has been scanned at the venue.

## 4. Using Tickets
1.  Click **"View Ticket"** on any event card.
2.  A QR Code will appear. Show this to the volunteer at the event entrance.
3.  **Share**: Use the "Share" button to send the registration link to your friends via WhatsApp!
