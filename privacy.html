Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 30, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name and email address (via Google Sign-In)
  • Event registration data
  • QR code scan history (for officers)
  • Support ticket information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process event registrations
  • Send you notifications about events
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Firebase services (Google Cloud Platform). We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Database, Hosting)
  • Google Sign-In (Authentication)

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our service and store certain information. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of your data
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Opt-out of push notifications

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@eventflow.edu
