Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for an event?

Login to your student account, browse upcoming events, and click "Buy Ticket" on any event you'd like to attend.

Where can I find my tickets?

After logging in, go to the "My Tickets" section on your student dashboard to view all your registered events and QR codes.

How do I become an admin?

Contact your college administrator to add your email to the admin whitelist.

Can I cancel my registration?

Currently, registrations cannot be cancelled. Please contact support if you need assistance.
